How it works

Simple structure, relaxed atmosphere, and lots of speaking time.

1) Warm-up

Short questions to switch into English and feel comfortable.

2) Topic discussion

Daily life themes + guided prompts to keep the conversation flowing.

3) Vocabulary

Useful phrases you can immediately reuse (not long lists).

4) Roleplay / debate

Breakout rooms in Zoom for pair/small-group practice.

5) Feedback

Quick wrap-up and suggestions for what to try next time.
